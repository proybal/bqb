About Us

Long ago while vacationing in Maui, Hawaii at a t-shirt shop I met a young newly wed couple from Kentucky. Having a conversation with the husband he asked where I was from to which i replied "Albuquerque". The wife across the store at another 'rounder shouted out "burque bro!". Finding out they had once traveled to the city and was told of the expression. Hence i created the website to honor the city I love and has been my home for over 40 years.

Here you will find links to places to go, things to see and stuff to buy in 'burque. The site is developed in Python/Django as a full-stack learning project.

I hope you enjoy the site and if so drop me a comment. Thanks, Paul Roybal
